CodeBash is a facebook messenger bot in the form of a facebook page which helps us to get notified about all the Hackathons,Coding Contest etc happening across platform like CodeChef, HackerEarth, Codeforces.Users can get CP ratings of any user from inside messenger by sending the username to the bot . You don't even need to visit their website , it all can be done by using Facebook messenger. Just SignUp and get all the notifications beforehand !!

How to Join

  • You can visit our Facebook page and send a message saying 'Join' to SignUp.
  • Or Click here and then in messenger Type "Join"
  • In the Messenger reply the bot with "Yes" or "No" for the sites you want updates.
  • To get ratings reply in messenger with"username" "website"
  • To unsubscribe just reply with "unsubscribe"
